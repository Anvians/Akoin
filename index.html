About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

We bridge the gap between academic insight—through our UK university partnership—and real-world projects to prepare the next generation of practitioners.

Our Model

Academy

Specialized training and bootcamps designed for students and professionals looking to pivot into risk management.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing intellectual property, future risk models, and robust AI governance frameworks.

Advisory Services

Helping organizations implement critical frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.

Who We Serve

We support a diverse community of students, early-career professionals, and practitioners in sectors like financial services and critical infrastructure seeking to upskill in digital and cyber risk.
